16. What's Next

Continuing the Journey

  • Video Language Models: How do we handle time? Temporal attention, sparse frame sampling, and keeping track of object permanence across 10,000 frames. Extremely High Difficulty.
  • Spatial UI Agents: Giving VLMs mouse control and the ability to click coordinates based on screen understanding (e.g., WebVoyager, Agentica). Very High Relevance.
  • Diffusion Models: The inverse operation—starting from text/noise and generating the image layout pixel by pixel using stable alignment architectures.
  • Parameter Efficient Fine Tuning (LoRA/QLoRA): How to train your own specific VLM for a highly niche task (e.g., reading x-rays or circuit boards) on a single consumer GPU.
